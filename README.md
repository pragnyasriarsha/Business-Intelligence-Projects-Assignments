# 📊 NHS Digital Transformation – Business Intelligence Report

## 📌 Overview
This project presents a detailed analysis of digital transformation in the NHS, focusing on the integration of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP), Business Intelligence (BI), and Business Process Management (BPM).

## 🎯 Objectives
- Evaluate ERP-enabled BI integration
- Analyse BPM strategies for healthcare improvement
- Examine UK data protection laws and ethical considerations

## 🔍 Key Topics Covered
- ERP systems and real-time dashboards
- Predictive analytics in healthcare
- Lean, Agile, and Six Sigma BPM approaches
- Patient-centred process redesign
- UK GDPR and ethical AI use

## 🛠 Tools & Concepts
- Business Intelligence
- Data Analytics
- Process Optimization
- Healthcare Systems
- Ethical Data Governance
